Privacy Policy — Last updated March 28, 2026

01

your data belongs to you

Everything you record in MyMarbles — your habits, your marbles, your reflections — lives only on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server or transmitted anywhere.

02

no collection, no tracking

MyMarbles contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party code that observes your behavior. We have no way to see your data and no interest in it.

03

built on Apple's own tools

Your information is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework, which keeps it private and local on your iPhone. No accounts are required to use the app.

  • No data collection
  • No tracking
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No accounts required
  • No server, ever
